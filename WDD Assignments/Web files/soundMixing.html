Sound Mixing Information

When you attend the sound mixing activity you will be shown how to use our complete home studio setup. This includes:

  • Computer
  • Digital Audio Workstation (DAW)
  • Audio Interface
  • Studio Monitors
  • Microphone & Microphone Stand
  • Studio Headphones
  • Quality Audio Cables
  • External or Backup Hard Drive
  • A Pop Filter
  • Sound Treatment

If you have an interest in music, this activity will teach you how it is created and edited. You will learn live sound engineering, recording techniques and mixing using music production software. You will also develop an understanding of different studio roles.
